Flavors

Sand, Stone & Sky

The sandstone visual style offers a light set of guidelines for page designs, but is not a set of absolute rules. A neutral flavor is provided by default, along with more distinct flavors for Mozilla pages and Firefox pages. Other sites and pages can be modified with their own distinct flavor.
